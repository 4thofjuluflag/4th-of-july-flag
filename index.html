Patriotic Pleated Fans

Pleated fans are one the most versatile options on the market. They can be hung from almost any surface so you're sure to find somewhere to hang them. While single fans can be displayed near focal points or doors, it is more common to display multiple fans with other decorations in order to make a larger display. To ensure that your display is successful, here are some guidelines to help you.

  • Symmetry. A balanced display using a consistent pattern will look better than any other. It's important to avoid using things that are not consistent with established patterns.
  • Simplicity. Complex designs can overwhelm viewers. Displays that have too many items at once can become ungainly and distract from the surrounding environment.
  • Coherency. Sticking to a theme is important for your display. While related decorations work well together, too many decorations can look chaotic and unorganized.

Bunting American Flag

While Fourth of July banners are visually striking, bunting is key to creating a set of party decorations. It can be used in all the same places as a pleated fan. However, bunting works best when it is connected to larger decorations. If there are a lot of flags, it will look empty. However, covering every inch with flags can make the display look cluttered and unpractical. Bunting can be used to connect the flags, filling in the gaps without any problems. Bunting is a great way to decorate spaces that require ornamentation but don't distract from the surrounding area. Bunting is subtle but can be used for decorations that are impossible to find with other methods.

American Flag Pulldowns

Although they require more skill than other Fourth of July banners they excel at their job. To ensure that the flags don't touch ground, they must be hung from a high location. This is why it's best to hang them from an awning or roof. These flags can be used to decorate areas from afar so the viewer can see all of them at once. They can be placed between windows, or used as a pair to flank doors. Pulldown flags will work best if there are no other decorations in these areas.

American Stick Flags

Stick flags are the smallest Fourth of Jul flags. Their size is their greatest advantage. Stick flags can be placed on tables and other furniture that is too small to decorate with banners or flags. They can also be used indoors where larger flags would be difficult to see. To ensure that the flags don't fall apart, you can either attach them to something strong or put them in a holder if you plan to use them outside.

These flags can be a great place to start for Fourth of July party ideas that will keep children entertained for a few hours.

  • Set up a few flags in your garden or house and let the kids hunt them.
  • Stick flags can be used to make festive batons for relay races or other athletic events.
  • A goody bag that children can take home is made by combining a stick flag, a small toy, and a few candy pieces.
